# Jyväskylä Cricket Club Website

Official website for the Jyväskylä Cricket Club (JCC), a registered Finnish sports association founded in 2009.

## About

The Jyväskylä Cricket Club is the first cricket club in the city of Jyväskylä, Finland. The club was officially registered as a Finnish association (ry) on June 3, 2009, with eleven founding members.

## Website Features

- **Home**: Club history and welcome information
- **Join Us**: Membership information and registration details
- **Fixtures**: Match schedules and results
- **Gallery**: Photos from club events and matches
- **Members**: Member directory
- **Contact**: Get in touch with the club
- **Expense Form**: For club financial management

## Training Information

- **Summer**: Outdoor training sessions at Kotalampi field in Keltinmäki, Jyväskylä (Sundays)
- **Winter**: Indoor sessions at the University's U1 sports hall (starting September)

The club welcomes both experienced cricket players and complete beginners.
